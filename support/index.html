Common questions

Answers before you wait for a reply.

How do I cancel a subscription?

Open Google Play, select your profile, then Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions → Ghost Inventory. Uninstalling the app does not cancel a subscription.

Why is Ghost Cloud unavailable on Free?

The Free plan stores inventory locally on the device. Encrypted Ghost Cloud backup and restore are included with Premium and Pro / Estate.

What happens if I downgrade?

Ghost is designed to preserve existing saved records. You can continue viewing, editing, exporting or deleting them, but creating new records can be blocked when the account is above the active plan limit.

Can support see my inventory?

Ghost Cloud is designed around encrypted private-vault backups. Support should not request or automatically receive your inventory content, photos, receipts, addresses or recovery keys during normal troubleshooting.

Can Ghost recover a lost vault passphrase?

Ghost Technologies does not store the plaintext recovery passphrase or a server-side key intended to decrypt an encrypted private-vault backup. If the passphrase is lost and no usable unlocked copy remains on a user-controlled device, the encrypted backup may be permanently unrecoverable.

What is included in a Full Evidence Report?

The Android launch build is designed to include an inventory summary plus readable selected item photos, receipt images and PDF pages, warranties, appraisals, certificates, ownership records, repair estimates and serial or VIN proof. Reports organize user-provided records and do not guarantee insurer acceptance or claim approval.

How do I delete my account?

Use Settings → Account → Delete Account in Ghost Inventory, or follow the instructions on the Delete Account page.
