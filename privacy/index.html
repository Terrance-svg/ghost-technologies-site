Privacy Policy

Effective Date: June 28, 2026

Ghost Inventory Lite is developed and operated by Ghost Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our application and related services.

Privacy-first approach

Ghost Inventory Lite is designed to keep inventory information on your device whenever possible. Certain optional features, including account access, AI Scan, Google Drive backup, subscriptions, and support, may require limited information to be transmitted so those features can function.

Information we collect

  • Email address and authentication information for account access.
  • Inventory information you create, such as properties, rooms, item names, categories, notes, serial numbers, estimated values, receipts, and photos.
  • Images you intentionally submit to AI Scan for item recognition.
  • Backup-related information when you choose to use Google Drive backup or restore.
  • Subscription status and purchase validation information processed through Google Play.
  • Support or feedback messages you choose to submit.

How we use information

  • To create and manage your account.
  • To store, organize, and display your inventory.
  • To provide AI-powered item recognition when you initiate AI Scan.
  • To provide backup and restore features when enabled.
  • To validate premium subscriptions through Google Play.
  • To provide support, improve reliability, and prevent abuse.

Local storage

Inventory data is stored locally on your device whenever possible. Photos and documents remain local unless you choose to use features that require upload, backup, or AI processing.

AI Scan

AI Scan only processes images you choose to submit. Images are used to help identify items and generate suggested inventory details. AI Scan is never performed automatically.

Google Drive backup

Google Drive backup is optional. If enabled, backup files are created for your account. Ghost Inventory Lite uses Google Drive permissions to access files created by the app. You may need to delete Google Drive backup files manually if you delete your account.

Purchases

Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play. Ghost Technologies does not collect or store your payment card details.

Account deletion

You may delete your account from inside the app. Account deletion removes server-side user-owned data associated with your account. Local data on your device and Google Drive backup files may require manual removal depending on your device and backup settings.

Data security

We use reasonable technical and organizational safeguards to protect information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or disclosure.

Children's privacy

Ghost Inventory Lite is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be posted on this page.
