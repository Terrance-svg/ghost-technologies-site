Privacy Policy

Ghost Inventory Lite is developed and operated by Ghost Technologies. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our app, website, and related services.

Local-first approach

Ghost Inventory Lite is designed to store inventory data locally on your device whenever possible. Some optional features, including account services, AI Scan, cloud backup, support, and subscriptions, require limited information to be transmitted so those features can function.

Information we may collect

  • Email address and account information used for authentication.
  • Inventory information that you enter, such as properties, rooms, item names, notes, serial numbers, values, receipts, and documents.
  • Photos and images you choose to add or submit for AI Scan.
  • Subscription status and purchase validation information processed through Google Play.
  • Support messages or feedback you choose to send.

How we use information

We use information to provide account access, store and organize inventory, process AI Scan requests, enable optional backup and restore, validate subscriptions, improve reliability, provide support, and prevent misuse.

AI Scan

AI Scan processes only images you intentionally submit for analysis. Images are not scanned automatically.

Backups

Cloud backup is optional. When enabled, backup features may transmit inventory data, photos, receipts, or documents to supported cloud services. Google Drive backups may require manual deletion from your Google Drive if you delete your account.

Payments

Premium subscriptions are processed through Google Play. Ghost Technologies does not collect or store your payment card information.

Data deletion

You may request or perform account deletion through the app where available. Account deletion removes server-side user-owned data associated with your account. Data stored on your device or in your own cloud backup location may need to be removed separately.

Children

Ghost Inventory Lite is not intended for children under 13 and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Contact

Questions can be sent through our contact page.
