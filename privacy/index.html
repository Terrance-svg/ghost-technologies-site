Legal & privacy

Privacy should be a product feature, not a footnote.

This policy explains how Ghost Inventory and Ghost Technologies handle account information, vault content, AI Scan processing, subscriptions and support requests.

1. Scope and who we are

This Privacy Policy applies to Ghost Inventory, the Ghost Technologies website, and support communications associated with the product. “Ghost,” “we,” and “us” refer to Ghost Technologies. Contact us at support@ghost-technologies.net.

Important: Ghost Inventory is designed so sensitive inventory content can be protected on the user’s device and, for paid cloud backup, encrypted before upload. No system can eliminate every risk, but data minimization and private-vault protection are core design goals.

2. Information Ghost may handle

Account and authentication information

  • Email address, user identifier, authentication status and account timestamps.
  • Information needed to support sign-in, password reset and account recovery.

Inventory and property content

  • Property and room records, item names, categories, values, descriptions, model and serial information.
  • Photos, receipts, warranties, notes, walkthrough media and generated reports that a user chooses to save.

Operational and diagnostic information

  • App version, device model, Android version, plan, usage allowance, storage usage and synchronization timestamps.
  • Error details that a user chooses to include in a support or bug report.

Billing information

Google Play processes payment-card and payment-account details. Ghost may receive subscription product identifiers, purchase tokens, entitlement state, renewal or expiration information and related billing metadata needed to verify access. Ghost does not receive a user’s complete payment-card number from Google Play.

3. How information is used

  • Provide account authentication and restore account sessions.
  • Store and organize the inventory records a user creates.
  • Generate reports, guide claim readiness and attach proof to items.
  • Process intentionally submitted AI Scan images and return suggested details.
  • Verify subscriptions, apply plan limits and restore paid access.
  • Provide encrypted cloud backup and recovery to eligible paid plans.
  • Respond to support, security and deletion requests.
  • Protect the service from abuse and troubleshoot failures.

Ghost Technologies does not sell personal information or use private vault content for targeted advertising.

4. Local vault and Ghost Cloud protection

Free-plan inventory is stored locally on the user’s device and does not include Ghost Cloud backup. Paid cloud backup is designed to encrypt sensitive vault content and attachments on the device before uploading them to private Supabase storage.

The user’s private-vault recovery material is intended to remain under user control. Ghost Technologies does not intentionally retain a server-side plaintext key that can unlock the encrypted inventory backup. Readable operational metadata may include account status, plan, usage counters, item or attachment counts, app version and timestamps needed to operate the service.

Users are responsible for protecting their device, account credentials and recovery passphrase. Losing recovery information may make encrypted backup data impossible to recover.

5. AI Scan processing

AI Scan is optional. When a signed-in user deliberately begins analysis, Ghost compresses and sends the selected image, requested detail level, image dimensions and currency preference through Ghost’s Supabase function to an AI service provider for item analysis. A monthly scan allowance is reserved when upload and analysis begin.

AI results can be incomplete or inaccurate. Users must review the suggested name, category, brand, model, serial information and value guidance before saving. AI value guidance is not an appraisal, insurance valuation or guarantee.

Ghost does not automatically send unrelated inventory records, property addresses, receipts, warranties or encryption keys with a normal AI Scan request.

6. Google Play subscriptions

Subscriptions are purchased and managed through Google Play. Google Play may provide Ghost with a purchase token and subscription status so Ghost can verify access through Google’s developer services. Ghost records the verified plan and entitlement state in the user’s account.

Subscriptions renew automatically unless cancelled through Google Play before the next renewal date. Uninstalling Ghost Inventory does not automatically cancel a subscription. Users can manage or cancel through Google Play Subscriptions.

7. Website and support communications

This website version does not include advertising trackers or analytics scripts and does not intentionally set tracking cookies. Standard hosting infrastructure may process basic network information such as IP address, browser type, requested page and timestamps for security and delivery.

When a user emails support, Ghost receives the email address, message and attachments the user chooses to send. The app’s bug-report flow may prepare privacy-safe device and version details, but it must not automatically attach inventory content, property addresses, photos, receipts, warranties or encryption keys.

8. Retention

Account and cloud data are retained while an account is active and as needed to provide requested services. Verified billing and security records may be retained when reasonably necessary for fraud prevention, accounting, dispute handling or legal obligations. Support correspondence may be retained to resolve the request and improve support quality.

Local inventory remains on the user’s device until the user deletes it, clears the app’s storage, or uninstalls the app in a manner that removes local app data.

9. Account and data deletion

Users can request deletion inside Ghost Inventory through the account settings or through the public Delete Account page. Account deletion removes the Ghost Cloud account, cloud backup, stored cloud attachments and Ghost entitlement record associated with the account, subject to limited records that must be retained for legal, billing, fraud-prevention or security reasons.

Deleting a Ghost account does not automatically cancel a Google Play subscription. Users must separately cancel the subscription through Google Play. Inventory stored only on the device may remain until the user deletes the local data or removes the app data.

10. Changes and contact

We may update this policy as Ghost Inventory changes. The effective date at the top will be revised when material updates are published.

Questions or privacy requests: support@ghost-technologies.net
